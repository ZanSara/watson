About Us

WHO ARE WE?

We're a team of students at the Politecnco di Milano working in the Data Science domain. Our goal with this App is to help users improving their style and suggest them new outfits in an easy and interactive way.

WHAT DO WE PROPOSE?

Our Idea is to understand the users' style from their photos posted on the social networks and recommend them interesting, new and cool clothes using Big Data analysis in the Fashion scope.
