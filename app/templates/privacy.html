Privacy Policy

Last Modified: May 28, 2017

FitYou is new way to change and improve your style. You can share with us photos from your instragram profile, or from your private directories. We will try to improve your outfit by analyzing most recent fashions.
Since your privacy means much to us, we would like you to learn precisely what we are going to do with the information that we collect from you. If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at FitYouTeam@gmail.com.

Information Collection and Use

We collect and use your information below to provide our Services and to measure and improve our Services from time to time.

Instagram contents

We have access to your profile info and media, especially we ask you to share with us some of the photos that you have shared on your profile. We have not access to your likes, settings or other private profile information.
We are not going to share your information with other users.

Private contents

We have access only to the images of your gallery that you choose. We can't access to other personal data present on your device.

Use

We use your pictures to understand your style and to provide you with good hints on how to improve it.
Your pictures will remain private and we won't use them after you have finished to use FitYou.
We make use of Ibm Watson Visual Recognition services then it is possible that some of your pictures are shared with Ibm services. Ibm, however, is not authorized to use them for other purpose than providing us with the services that we ask for.
We could make use of your activity on our app in order to improve our services and provide better user experiences.

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure we have put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Future changes

Changes to this PolicyWe may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version of the policy will govern our use of your information and will always be at: http://eiteam-project-eiteam-project.7e14.starter-us-west-2.openshiftapps.com/privacy.
